Our Vision

Our vision at Thymos Science Academy is to set for education in the twenty-first century. Thymos Science Academy is an amazing school where you can excel in academics and also build friendships that will last a lifetime. Thymos Science Academy is the only academic and non-academic School in our area. This drives us even more to begin with the end in mind, and prepare students for life-long success and careers of impact.

Our Mission

The Mission of Thymos Science Academy is to develop intellectually curious and capable young people who are selfless contributors to both local and global communities, and who aspire to be ethical and compassionate leaders. Through a rigorous and innovative academic program in math, science, and technology, in combination with the liberal arts, Thymos Science Academy affords its diverse student population those experiences, skills, and values that will prepare them for purposeful contributions in higher education and STEM-related fields.

Our Services

At Thymos Science Academy, we are committed to providing a comprehensive array of services designed to meet the diverse needs of our students and their families. Our goal is to offer a holistic educational experience that goes beyond traditional academics, empowering students to thrive both inside and outside the classroom.

Academic Programs:

  • Core Subjects: We offer a rigorous curriculum in core subjects such as mathematics, science, language arts, and social studies, ensuring that students develop a strong foundation in essential academic areas.
  • Enrichment Courses: Our enrichment programs encompass a wide range of subjects including coding, robotics, creative writing, foreign languages, music, and art, allowing students to explore their interests and develop new skills.
  • Advanced Placement (AP) Courses: For high school students seeking a challenge, we offer Advanced Placement courses that provide college-level coursework and the opportunity to earn college credit while still in high school.

Personalized Learning:

  • Small Class Sizes: With small class sizes, our students receive personalized attention from our experienced educators, fostering a supportive learning environment where every student can thrive.
  • Individualized Instruction: We recognize that each student has unique learning needs and preferences. Our teachers tailor instruction to accommodate diverse learning styles, ensuring that every student receives the support they need to succeed.

Academic Support Services:

  • Tutoring: We offer tutoring services in various subjects to provide additional support and reinforcement for students who may need extra help.
  • Study Skills Workshops: Our study skills workshops equip students with essential strategies and techniques to improve their organization, time management, note-taking, and test-taking skills.

College and Career Preparation:

  • College Counseling: Our college counseling services guide students through the college application process, offering support with college selection, application essays, standardized testing, and financial aid.
  • Career Exploration: We provide resources and opportunities for students to explore different career paths, gain real-world experience through internships, and develop the skills necessary for success in their chosen fields.

Extracurricular Activities:

  • Sports Teams: Our athletic programs offer opportunities for students to participate in a variety of sports, promoting physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship.
  • Clubs and Organizations: We offer a diverse range of clubs and organizations where students can pursue their interests, develop leadership skills, and connect with peers who share similar passions.

Community Engagement:

  • Service Learning Projects: Through service learning projects, students have the opportunity to make a positive impact in their communities while developing empathy, compassion, and a sense of civic responsibility.
  • Parent Involvement: We actively involve parents in their child's education through regular communication, parent-teacher conferences, workshops, and volunteer opportunities.

At Thymos Science Academy, we are dedicated to providing exceptional services that empower students to achieve academic excellence, personal growth, and success in all areas of life. Contact us today to learn more about how we can support your child's educational journey!
